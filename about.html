About Me!

Hello! My name is Nhan Nguyen. The simple pronounciation of my name is "Na-an Wh-in". Its quite a simple name to pronounce, but can throw people off because of the spelling. I'm a 1st generation American with Vietnamese ancestors with my parents being the first to arrive in the states! A little about myself: I'm 29, going on 30 with strong interests in software development. When I was a kid, I had strong interests in games. Spending hours and hours, learning the different worlds that others created for me was so interesting. I had nothing but respect for the story tellers of the digital age. In my adolescence and late teens, learning to code never became reality. My schools never taught it as a class and I never found a chance to learn. In my early adult life, I enlisted in the military. "The world is my oyster"... so I thought.
